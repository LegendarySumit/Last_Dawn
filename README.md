## 🧠 Concept

One day, without warning, the sky darkens.

People vanish — adults first, then children, then entire families.  
Anyone exposed to the outside world disappears.

**You survive by accident.**

An ancient artifact binds itself to you, granting resistance to the corruption beyond closed doors.

As you step into the abandoned world, you uncover the truth behind the abductions, the sentinels guarding the land, and the power controlling it all.

---

## 🎮 Features

✅ **Act-Based Narrative Progression** — Story unfolds in structured chapters  
✅ **Dark Fantasy / Post-Apocalyptic Atmosphere** — Heavy, quiet, inevitable  
✅ **Cinematic, Story-Driven Combat** — Non-mechanical, narrative-focused  
✅ **Boss Encounters** — Multi-stage animations and staged confrontations  
✅ **Custom Sprite Positioning** — Precise scene composition via `transforms.rpy`  
✅ **Character-Focused Dialogue** — Deliberate pacing and weight  
✅ **Ambient Audio Design** — Sound and music create atmosphere

## ⚠️ Technical Notes

- Asset names are **case-sensitive**
- Scene layout is controlled via `transforms.rpy`
- Boss scenes reserve **center space** by design
- Combat is **narrative-driven, not mechanical**
- All positioning and staging is custom-defined

## 📌 Vision

**LastDawn** is designed to feel **quiet, heavy, and inevitable** —

A story where:
- **Silence matters**
- **Survival has weight**
- **Every encounter pushes the world closer to its final dawn**

This is not a power fantasy. This is about **what remains when everything else is gone.**

---

## 🎭 Design Philosophy

- **Atmosphere over action** — Mood carries the narrative
- **Cinematic composition** — Every scene is staged deliberately
- **Weight in dialogue** — Words matter, silence matters more
- **Narrative combat** — Fights are stories, not mechanics
- **Inevitable progression** — The world moves toward its end
